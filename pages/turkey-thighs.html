Juicy Oven Baked Turkey Thighs

  1. Cut the stem

    2. This way you will stay with the meat of the broccoli

  2. Boil the water

    3. Find a catlle that will fit all the broccoli flowers

  3. Boil broccoli for two minutes

    4. I am usually boiling broccoli in order to "wash" it

  4. Place broccoli on the baking tray

    5. There should be a bit of space within broccoli flowers

  5. Pour olive oil over the broccoli

    6. This way you add healthy fats to this healthy vegetable

  6. Bake for 15 minutes
